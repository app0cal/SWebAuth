LA Fitness Olympic Bar Review

By: Marcos Sanchez | Date: March 29, 2026

Figure 1: Olympic bar at LA Fitness photographed by Marcos Sanchez

Rating

Pros and Cons

Pros: Good grip, comfortable ring placement, works well for bench and squat setup

Cons: Bars can wear down over time, heavier lifters may notice limitations

The Olympic bar at LA Fitness is one of the better basic gym bars to use because of how comfortable it feels during common lifts. The ring placement is in a really good spot for bench pressing and unracking squats, which makes the setup feel easier and more natural.

Another good thing about it is the grip. Compared to some other bars, it feels better in the hands and does not feel as awkward when trying to stay locked in during a set. That matters more on exercises where bar control is important.

One of the weaker points is that this is still just a standard gym bar, so as the weight gets much heavier, some people might start to notice its limits. That is not a common problem for most people, but it is still worth mentioning.

Overall, this is a strong piece of gym equipment for most lifters. For a regular commercial gym bar, it works well and does what it needs to do.

StairMaster Review

By: Marcos Sanchez | Date: March 28, 2026

Figure 2: StairMaster machine photographed by Marcos Sanchez

Product Information

Item: StairMaster

Type: Cardio machine

Best for: Cardio, calorie burn, shorter workouts

Rating

★★★★☆ (4 out of 5)

Pros and Cons

Pros: Efficient cardio, burns calories quickly, good when short on time

Cons: Can be overused, not always ideal depending on training goals

Access Links

LA Fitness official site

StairMaster brand site

The StairMaster is one of the more popular cardio machines because of how efficient it is. It is good for burning calories in a shorter amount of time, which is why a lot of people use it when they are in a rush or trying to fit cardio into a lifting day.

One of the biggest strengths of the StairMaster is that it feels intense pretty quickly. You do not need a very long session to feel like you got solid work in, and that makes it useful for people trying to save time.

At the same time, it is not perfect in every context. Depending on someone’s goals and how much fatigue they can recover from, it might not always be the best machine to use often. It can be harder on the legs compared to some other cardio options.

Overall, the StairMaster is a really effective machine, but it works best when used with a purpose. It is strongest as a tool for efficient cardio rather than something everyone should automatically use all the time.
